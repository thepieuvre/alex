The Pieuvre

Reading the Internet

http://thepieuvre.com

  2013

Click for expanding details...

Pieuvre [pjœvʀ] Pieuvre is the french word for octopus. You can learn more about this fantastic animal at Wikipedia.

Today, the Internet provides so many sources of information, so many topics, so many different opinions, etc. It requires an important time to follow all this information.

The Pieuvre aims to provide a new way of organising and reading information on/of the Internet. It organises and finds the information for you, by reading the Internet.

This is a News Reader (RSS reader) based on natural language processing for extracting keywords and grouping articles by similarities. Technologies: Python NTLK, Redis, Grails/Groovy, ...
